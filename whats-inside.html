Data & Methods

  • Sources: NSE/BSE for prices, volumes, corporate actions; official indices.
  • Cleaning: Survivorship-aware panels for backtests; split/bonus adjustments.
  • Smoothing: Trailing averages (3/6/12-M), winsorization for extreme outliers.
  • Publishing: Units, baselines, and caveats posted; see Methodology & Data Sources.
