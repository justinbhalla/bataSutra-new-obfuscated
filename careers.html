Role Team Type Location Summary Apply

Don’t see a fit? Send a short note + links to careers@batasutra.in.

Culture

  • Pragmatic craft: Fewer, better; measured by impact, not output.
  • High trust, low ego: Direct feedback, clear ownership.
  • Reader first: Utility, clarity, and accuracy over hot takes.

How we work

  • Hybrid-first (India) with async collaboration.
  • Documentation > meetings; lightweight rituals.
  • Focus blocks and no-meeting windows.

Benefits

Health & Insurance
Group health cover incl. dependents; mental-health stipend.
Time Off
Generous leave, optional company shutdowns, flexible holidays.
Learning
Annual learning budget; books, courses, conferences.
Gear
Quality laptop/peripherals; home office stipend.
Parental
Inclusive parental leave; flexible return.
Ownership
Meaningful responsibility and career growth.

How We Hire

  1. Share CV/portfolio + 2–3 work samples via careers@batasutra.in.
  2. Intro call (30 min): team, role context, Q&A.
  3. Practical task aligned to the role (2–4 hrs async).
  4. Panel: craft deep-dive + values interview.
  5. Offer & mutual references.

Teams

  • Editorial: Markets, policy, explainers, data-led storytelling.
  • Data: ETL, dashboards, QA, and methodology.
  • Design: Systems, brand, product, and graphics.
  • Engineering: Frontend, performance, infra-light services.
  • Growth: Newsletters, partnerships, performance channels.

Tools we like

TypeScript, Tailwind, simple build pipelines, reproducible notebooks, and great docs.

Internships

Editorial, data, and design internships run year-round. 8–16 weeks, stipend provided. Write to careers@batasutra.in with “Internship — [Team]”.

Referrals

Know someone perfect? Email careers@batasutra.in with the role, their LinkedIn/portfolio, and a short note.

Equal Opportunity

bataSutra is an equal-opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on caste, religion, gender identity/expression, sexual orientation, age, disability, veteran status, or any legally protected status. We provide reasonable accommodations during hiring—write to careers@batasutra.in.
