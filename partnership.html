Partnership models

  • Branded Content Story series, explainers, and interviews aligned to your objectives and our editorial standards.
  • Data Collaborations Co-built indices, trackers, and dashboards; attribution and methods published.
  • Research & Whitepapers Original surveys or literature reviews with charts and executive briefs.
  • Events & Workshops Private roundtables or public webinars; agenda, speakers, and collateral handled end-to-end.
  • API & Tools Embeddable calculators or data APIs with SLAs where applicable.

Lead times

  • Quick explainer: 1–2 weeks
  • Series/whitepaper: 3–6 weeks
  • Dashboard/API: scoped

Featured offerings

Three-part series unpacking a complex topic with charts & Q&A.

Sector Dashboard

C-Suite Roundtable

Invite-only session with briefing doc and post-event summary.

How we work

  1. Discovery: goals, audience, message, KPIs.
  2. Scope: formats, timeline, approvals, deliverables.
  3. Production: reporting, design, engineering.
  4. Distribution: on-site + socials + partner channels.
  5. Reporting: performance review and next steps.

What we need

Brief, target audience, examples you like, success criteria, and any constraints.

KPIs & reporting

  • Reach & reads: unique visitors, completion rates.
  • Engagement: time-on-page, scroll depth, shares.
  • Outcomes: signups, qualified leads (where applicable).

Brand safety & disclosure

Partners receive clear “Sponsored” labels and disclosures. Editorial standards apply to all outputs; data sources and methods are cited. See Editorial Policy and Ethics & Independence.

Compliance

We follow applicable advertising, attribution, and data-protection norms.

FAQ

Do you share raw leads?

We can support qualified interest capture via forms with user consent and agreed fields.

How are approvals handled?

We align on a lightweight approval matrix and deadlines at kickoff.
