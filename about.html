About

bataSutra is an India-focused publication that pairs reporting with data and practical tools. We try to be useful first — to investors, operators, and policy watchers.

Mission How we work Team Contact
At a glance
  • Launch: 2025

  • Focus: Business, markets, policy
  • Contact: hello@batasutra.in

Mission

Make India’s economy easier to understand — and act on — through clear reporting, transparent data, and usable tools.

Clarity
Explain complex topics with context and charts that respect the data.
Pragmatism
Favor what works over what sounds good; quantify where possible.
Integrity
Independence in coverage; clear disclosures; fast corrections.

How we work

  • Data first: we publish sources, methods, and caveats where possible. See Data Sources and Methodology.
  • Separation of church & state: our Ethics & Compliance and Editorial Policy govern coverage.
  • Corrections welcomed: see Corrections and Responsible Disclosure.
  • Tools, not gimmicks: calculators and dashboards are built to answer real questions, quickly.

What we publish

  • Daily briefs & explainers
  • Data dashboards & trackers
  • Investor/operator tools
  • Long-form “Deep Dives”

Team

We’re a small, interdisciplinary team across reporting, data, product, and design. We collaborate with external experts and advisors as needed.

Editorial

Product & Design

Standards
