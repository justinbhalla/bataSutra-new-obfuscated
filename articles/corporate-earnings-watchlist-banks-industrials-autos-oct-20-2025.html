Call checklists by sector

BFSI (SBI Cards, SBI Life, ABSL AMC, Geojit)

  • Cards: revolve share, credit costs, transacting vs payment-only mix.
  • Life: VNB margin, product mix (non-par/annuity), persistency bands.
  • AMC: SIP flows, equity mix, expense-ratio discipline.
  • Brokers: active clients, blended take-rate, opex leverage.

FMCG/Staples (HUL, Colgate)

  • Price vs volume; rural/urban split; gross margin bridge (RM, mix, pricing).
  • Ad-spend normalisation; modern trade vs GT; innovation pipeline.

IT/Analytics (Coforge, eClerx, Latent View)

  • Large-deal TCV with ramp dates; price discipline; subcontracting drag.
  • Vertical mix (BFSI/travel) and cross-currency impact.

Healthcare (Dr. Reddy’s, Laurus)

  • US launches, biosimilar/complex ramp; price-erosion cadence.
  • India branded growth; CM2 after freight/energy.

Industrials/Chemicals (Vardhman, Sagar Cements, Supreme Petrochem)

  • Utilisation and spreads; RM pass-through; energy sensitivity.
  • Order/price discipline and export traction where relevant.
