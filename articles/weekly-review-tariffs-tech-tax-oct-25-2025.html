The short

  • Tariffs: Scrutiny on borderline HS codes rose this week; importers should lock broker pre-clearances and pass-through clauses.
  • GST: Clarifications on composite items require invoice & ERP changes; run a relabel plan to avoid dual-MRP friction.
  • AI/DC: Fresh DRHPs from data-centre and renewables names signal sustained infra demand; verify power/water SLAs before capex.
  • Households: Fuel city-bands broadly stable m/m; staples remain the volatility driver in urban CPI baskets.
  • Play: Advance-purchase critical imports; phase DC builds (shell-first); keep dual-rail eKYC in case of API churn.
