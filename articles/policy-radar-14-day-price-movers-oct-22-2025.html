Policy Radar (Next 14 Days): What Can Actually Change Prices

Tariffs, taxes, auctions and compliance that move P&L. Who’s impacted, what to file, and how fast changes bite.
By bataSutra Editorial · October 22, 2025
In this piece:
  • The short — price-moving levers in two weeks
  • Calendar view: 14-day watchlist
  • Regulator cards: SEBI · RBI · IRDAI · UIDAI · CBIC
  • Compliance actions (Owner · Doc · Due)
  • Risk matrix & hedges

The short

  • Tariffs & GST: Even small rate edits ripple through MRP/realisation—BOM repricing in 3–7 days.
  • Auctions & quotas: Calendar shifts change landed cost and inventory strategy faster than price caps.
  • Market rules: Disclosure/settlement tweaks affect float/liquidity—watch free float and lock-ins.
  • Data & ID rails: eKYC/API changes alter onboarding cost per customer within a week.
  • Banking rails: LCR/NSFR/advisories push funding costs—cards/BNPL risk limits tighten first.

Regulator cards

SEBI — primary & market plumbing

  • Observation timelines & disclosure granularity (unit economics, RPT).
  • Float/lock-in tweaks → day-2 liquidity profile.

RBI — funding & rails

  • LCR/NSFR and unsecured growth guardrails; card/BNPL risk advisories.
  • UPI/RTGS operational notes that move failure/chargeback rates.

IRDAI — protection economics

  • Product filing norms; persistency disclosures; expense caps.

UIDAI & digital public goods

  • eKYC cost/limit changes; API spec updates; offline KYC flows.

CBIC (Customs/GST)

  • Rate/valuation clarifications; exemptions sunset/renewal windows.
