Consumer Price Tracker: Food, Fuel & Fees

City fuel bands, grocery basket drift, fee watch, and quick switches to keep your monthly spend in line.
By bataSutra Editorial · October 19, 2025
In this piece:
  • The short — three levers this week
  • Fuel bands by city + car math
  • Grocery basket: weekly drift (illustrative)
  • Fees to watch: banking, telecom, travel

The short

  • Fuel: Retail bands steady across metros; diesel cracks—not Brent—drive risk to stability.
  • Food: Pulses and edible oils set the tone; onions/tomatoes volatile by mandi and state levies.
  • Fees: Late-fee and convenience-charge creep can offset EMI savings; pick zero-fee rails where possible.

Guardrails

  • Watch 2–3 week trend in Brent & diesel crack spreads—more predictive than 24-hr spikes.
  • If INR weakens by ₹1, add ~₹0.50–₹0.70/L pressure to retail math.

Fees to watch — banking, telecom, travel

Banking/fintech

  • ATM/branch cash charges—switch to UPI/NEFT windows.
  • Late fees on BNPL/credit cards—set auto-pay with buffer.

Telecom

  • Data pack creep—audit “family” bundles vs single lines.
  • International roaming—use eSIM or daily pass only as needed.

Travel

  • Convenience fees—book via airline apps; avoid meta search checkouts for payment charges.
  • Baggage add-ons—pre-book; airport purchases are costlier.
Bands and drifts are directional as of Oct 19, 2025. Use your city’s posted prices and store apps to finalize budgets.
