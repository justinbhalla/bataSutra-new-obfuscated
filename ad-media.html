Sponsorships & Custom

  • Series sponsor Branded presence across a themed editorial series (logo lockup, intro/outro tag, CTA link).
  • Newsletter sponsor Category exclusivity for an issue; header card + midroll mention.
  • Tool/Index sponsor “Supported by” on dashboards/tools with evergreen attribution.
  • Custom research Survey/data project with co-branded report and launch article.

Lead times

Display: 5 business days • Newsletter: 3–5 • Custom: 2–4 weeks

Brand Safety & Guidelines

  • We do not run ads with misleading claims, illegal products, or discriminatory targeting.
  • Sponsored content is clearly labeled and adheres to our Editorial Policy.
  • Tracking is minimal and privacy-respecting; see Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.
