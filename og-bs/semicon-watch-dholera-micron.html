Semicon Watch: Where Tata’s Dholera Fab & Micron Sanand Are Now

Milestones achieved, what’s next on the Gantt, hiring timelines, and how the local ecosystem is shaping up around Gujarat’s chip corridor.
By bataSutra Editorial · September 3, 2025
In this piece:
  • The short: status at a glance
  • Tata–PSMC (Dholera): capex, capacity, nodes, timeline
  • Micron (Sanand ATMP): cleanroom, hiring, first-outs
  • Local impact: vendors, housing, talent pipelines
  • FAQ: What’s realistic for 2025–26?

The short

  • Tata–PSMC Fab (Dholera): Cabinet-approved project of ~₹91,000 cr with planned 50,000 WSPM (300 mm) capacity; construction underway after the Sept ’24 tech-transfer agreement.
  • Micron ATMP (Sanand): Phase-1 cleanroom validation reported mid-2025; hiring ramp targets ~5,000 direct jobs and ~15,000 community jobs over time.
  • Outlook 2025–26: Dholera moves through build-out and tool-install; Micron scales ATMP output with export focus once validation transitions to volume.

Micron (Sanand ATMP): where things stand

Timeline

  • Project announced: Jun 2023; Phase-1 ops originally targeted late 2024.
  • Cleanroom validation stage: Jun 2025.
  • First export-bound output window signalled for 2025 as validation moves to ramp.

People & capability

  • ~5,000 direct Micron jobs; ~15,000 community jobs over the next several years.
  • SEZ approvals and ongoing capex underpin export orientation in early phases.
What ATMP means: Assembly, test, mark, and pack—i.e., post-fab packaging and final test. It’s not wafer fabrication; that’s what Tata–PSMC brings at Dholera.

Local ecosystem impacts (Gujarat focus)

  • Vendors & logistics: Tool-install and facilities O&M pull in specialty gases, ultrapure water systems, cleanroom EPC, and third-party metrology.
  • Talent: Hiring spans facilities, yield, test, and reliability, with Dholera fab roles in MES/automation also appearing.
  • Spillovers: OSAT pilots (Sanand) and allied projects increase demand for substrate, leadframe, and molding compound suppliers in-country.

FAQ

  • Will Dholera make bleeding-edge chips? No—initial focus is mature nodes (e.g., 28/40/55 nm) where auto/industrial demand is deep and supply tightness matters.
  • Is Micron “making chips” in India? It’s packaging and testing chips (ATMP). Wafer fabrication remains outside India (for now).
  • When do we see volume? Expect a staged ramp: Micron ATMP through 2025; Dholera fab toward 2026 and beyond.
