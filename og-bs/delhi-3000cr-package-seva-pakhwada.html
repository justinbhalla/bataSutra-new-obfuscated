Delhi’s ₹3,000-cr Package: Drainage, Water, Health, Roads — What’s Actually Launching

Seva Pakhwada (Sep 17–Oct 2): Drainage master plan, Okhla STP upgrades, Nand Nagri flyover, 101 Ayushman centres, MRI & dialysis units, and more.
By bataSutra Editorial · September 12, 2025
In this piece:
  • What’s in the ₹3,000-cr package (quick view)
  • Health, water & sewage: where capacity gets added
  • Mobility upgrades (flyovers/FOBs) & flood resilience
  • What to watch through October

The short

  • When: Rollouts begin September 17 during Seva Pakhwada (till Oct 2).
  • Ticket size: ~₹3,000 crore spanning water, sewage, drainage, roads, and health.
  • Flagships: Drainage master plan (city-wide), Okhla STP upgrades, Nand Nagri flyover, 101 Ayushman centres, 38 MRI & 150 dialysis units.
  • Context: Packaged around PM’s birthday (Sep 17) as part of a service fortnight; more launches expected through the window.

Health: access & diagnostics

  • Operationalise 38 MRI machines and 150 dialysis units across key hospitals.
  • Launch 101 Ayushman Arogya centres to expand primary care reach.
  • Upgrades at Acharya Shree Bhikshu & Sanjay Gandhi Memorial hospitals.

Water, sewage & drainage

  • Okhla STP and network strengthening in Sonia Vihar and other zones.
  • City-wide drainage master plan to address monsoon flooding hotspots.
  • Parallel push on Yamuna floodplain demarcation starting Sep 15 to curb encroachment.

Mobility & urban works

  • Nand Nagri flyover to de-bottleneck traffic in Northeast Delhi.
  • New FOB near Rajputana Rifles campus; carriageway repairs & resurfacing.

Who benefits first

  • Flood-prone wards (drainage & pumping capacity adds).
  • Dialysis/MRI wait-listed patients; peri-urban neighbourhoods with weak primary care.
  • Commuters on corridors feeding Nand Nagri and adjacent nodes.

What to watch (Sep 17 → Oct 2)

  1. Commissioning pace: Are MRI/dialysis units staffed & live, not just announced?
  2. Drainage works: Tendering vs execution—spot checks in chronic water-logging zones.
  3. Floodplain demarcation: Boards up by Sep 15? Immediate encroachment response?

Civic lens Use ward-level trackers (complaints, pump uptime) to audit delivery through the monsoon tail.

Details reflect official statements and major dailies as of Sep 12, 2025. Execution timelines can shift; verify ward-level notices before citing exact commissioning dates.
