Top 5 Stories You Missed This Week

By bataSutra Editorial Team | August 3, 2025

India’s economic pulse kept beating strong this week with key reforms, shifting alliances, and billion-dollar moves. In case you unplugged, here’s what stood out:

1. RBI Proposes Licensing Framework for Fintechs

The Reserve Bank of India released a draft framework to license and regulate non-bank fintech firms, citing systemic risk and customer data security. Startups worry the move may raise entry barriers, while banks cheer greater oversight.

2. IndiaStack Expands Globally with Singapore, Kenya Onboard

India formalized agreements with Singapore and Kenya to share components of IndiaStack, including UPI and DigiLocker. The initiative is seen as a soft-power play and a step toward cross-border digital public infrastructure (DPI) diplomacy.

3. Ola Electric’s IPO Filing Reveals ₹2,600 Cr Revenue

Ola Electric filed its DRHP with SEBI, revealing ₹2,600 crore in FY25 revenue and narrowed losses. With delivery ramp-ups and government subsidies, the EV unicorn aims to list before Diwali — possibly India’s biggest green IPO yet.

4. Merchandise Exports Rebound 9.2% in July

India’s merchandise exports rose 9.2% YoY in July after six months of contraction, led by electronics, chemicals, and agri-products. Analysts attribute the surge to improved logistics and easing Red Sea tensions.

5. Adani Green Bags ₹10,000 Cr Solar Infra Contract

Adani Green Energy won a ₹10,000 crore government contract to develop solar power parks in Rajasthan and Gujarat. The move strengthens Adani’s renewable credentials amid continued scrutiny over group debt levels.
