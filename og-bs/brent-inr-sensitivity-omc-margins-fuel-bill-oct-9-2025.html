Household planner

  • For a 1,200 km/month driver at 14 km/L: petrol spend ≈ ₹8,700–9,000 at Delhi bands.
  • Same run on diesel at 18 km/L: ≈ ₹6,000–6,200.
  • Tip: Small route/traffic optimizations (−5% kms) save ~₹300–450/month.

EV cross-check (if relevant)

  • At ₹9/kWh and 7 km/kWh: ≈ ₹1,540 for 1,200 km (ex-parking/financing).
  • Use your city’s slab and charger tariff to sanity-check.

What can change prices next

  • Unexpected crude supply headlines shifting Brent by ±$5–10/bbl.
  • INR volatility around large FX outflows or global risk events.
  • State/central tax tweaks; OMC retail policy windows around festivals.

Operator tips — fleets/SMEs

  • Use fuel cards with weekly caps; reconcile mileage anomalies.
  • Hedge diesel exposure via staggered vendor contracts; add buffer for long-haul corridors.
  • Schedule refuels in lower-band cities when routes allow.

Operator tips — households

  • Refuel mid-week mornings; avoid weekend/holiday congestion losses.
  • Tyre pressure & basic service can deliver 3–5% efficiency gains.
