FASTag Annual Pass: ₹3,000 Highway Plan — Savings & Reality

1 year or 200 toll-plaza crossings (whichever first). Who qualifies, how to activate, and a break-even planner you can apply to your route.
By bataSutra Editorial · August 19, 2025
In this piece:
  • What the Annual Pass covers and who’s eligible
  • Activation steps (RajmargYatra/NHAI)
  • Break-even planner (round trips & plazas)
  • Who should/shouldn’t buy; edge cases

The short

  • What: A ₹3,000 FASTag Annual Pass for non-commercial vehicles; valid for one year or 200 toll-plaza crossings, whichever comes first.
  • Where: ~1,150 toll plazas on National Highways & National Expressways (NHAI network).
  • How: Activate via the RajmargYatra app or NHAI website; typical activation within ~2 hours.
  • Uptake: 5 lakh+ users in the first four days since the Aug 15 launch; southern states led early adoption.

Who actually saves

Rule: Each plaza crossing counts toward the 200 cap. If a round trip touches N plazas per direction, that’s 2N crossings per round trip. Heavier highway users exhaust the pass faster and save more.

Good fit

  • Intercity commuters (sales, services) on NH/expressways
  • Frequent airport/industrial-corridor users (private LMVs)
  • Families doing regular weekend drives across states

Probably skip

  • Occasional highway users (< 1 round trip/month)
  • City-only drivers (few or zero NH plazas)
  • Commercial vehicles (not eligible)

Activation — step by step

  1. Check eligibility: Private/non-commercial LMV with an active FASTag and KYC done.
  2. Open RajmargYatra app (or NHAI website) → Annual Pass → enter vehicle/FASTag details.
  3. Pay ₹3,000 (one-time). Activation typically reflects within ~2 hours.
  4. Drive as usual: Crossings on participating NH/expressway plazas auto-debit from your Annual Pass balance (until 200 crossings or 12 months).

Tip Track crossings in the app to avoid surprises near the 200-crossing limit.

Fine print & edge cases

  • Coverage: Applicable on ~1,150 National Highway & National Expressway plazas onboarded to the scheme; state/private roads may not be covered.
  • Validity: Ends on the earlier of 12 months or 200 crossings. After that, normal tolls apply.
  • Vehicle-bound: Tied to your FASTag/vehicle; non-transferable.
  • Commercial use: The launch is for non-commercial vehicles.

FAQ

  • Is it “unlimited”? No. It’s capped at 200 toll-plaza crossings or 1 year, whichever comes first.
  • What if toll rates change mid-year? Your cap is by crossings, not rupee amount; rate changes don’t affect the 200 limit.
