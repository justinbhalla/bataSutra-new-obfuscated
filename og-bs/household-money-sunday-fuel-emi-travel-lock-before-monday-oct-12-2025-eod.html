The short

  • Fuel: Retail bands steady in top metros; use city table for planning.
  • EMIs: Lending rates broadly stable; prepaying 1–2 EMIs a year can shave months off tenure.
  • Travel: For non-peak routes, ~14–21 day advance often beats last-minute buys; lock early-morning departures.
  • Utilities: Check your electricity slab before festive purchases—appliances can bump you a tier.

Car math & quick savings (Delhi band)

Car math

  • Monthly 1,000 km at 14 km/L (petrol) → ₹7,250–7,500.
  • Monthly 1,000 km at 18 km/L (diesel) → ₹5,000–5,200.
  • EV at 7 km/kWh, ₹9/kWh → ~₹1,285 (ex-parking/financing).

Quick savings

  • Tyre pressure + route planning → 3–5% fuel cut.
  • Shift 1 errand to weekday → saves traffic idle burn.

Airfare timing & hotel hacks

Flights

  • Non-peak routes: book 2–3 weeks ahead; early-morning departures price better and run on time.
  • Carry-on only for short trips; avoid last-minute add-on fees.

Hotels

  • Tier-2 city weekends can be cheaper—check business districts.
  • Confirm final tariff with taxes/fees; look for breakfast-included plans.

Weekly checklist

Family

  • Fuel top-up mid-week morning; avoid holiday queues.
  • Review electricity slab; schedule heavy-use chores off-peak.
  • Set a grocery budget and pre-order staples online.

Small business

  • Reprice delivery surcharge if diesel crosses your threshold.
  • Chase receivables & reconcile GST before mid-month.
  • Back up POS/CRM and rotate passwords for vendor logins.
