Standards & Commitment

  • We track WCAG 2.2 AA and ARIA Authoring Practices where applicable.
  • Semantic HTML first; ARIA only when needed to enhance semantics.
  • Accessible names, roles, and states are exposed to assistive tech.

Scope

Core marketing pages, article experiences, and tools. Some third-party embeds may vary.

Keyboard & Focus

  • Logical tab order with visible focus styles.
  • Skip links and landmark regions for faster navigation.
  • No keyboard traps; modals and menus cycle focus appropriately.

Color, Contrast & Typography

  • Text contrast meets AA (4.5:1; 3:1 for large text) or better.
  • Color never conveys meaning alone; additional cues provided.
  • Resizable text with responsive layouts; no loss of content.

Media: Alt Text, Captions, Transcripts

  • Images include descriptive alt text or are marked decorative.
  • Video content includes captions; audio has transcripts when applicable.
  • Charts include data tables or descriptive summaries.

Forms & Validation

  • Every input has an associated label; errors are announced to AT.
  • Clear instructions, examples, and inline validation where possible.
  • Touch targets sized for mobile; no reliance on fine motor precision.

Tables & Data Visuals

  • Tables use proper headers, scopes, and captions.
  • Complex tables provide summaries or alternate views.

Motion & Animation

  • Respects prefers-reduced-motion; essential animations minimized.
  • No flashing content exceeding recommended thresholds.

Testing & Audits

  • Automated checks + manual keyboard/reader passes on key flows.
  • Defects are tracked and prioritized; regressions are blocked.

Support & Feedback

Need help or found an accessibility issue? Email accessibility@batasutra.in with the page URL, steps, and assistive tech details.

Alternate formats

On request, we can provide alternative formats for select stories or data tables.

Changes

We may update this page. Material changes will reflect a new “Last updated” date above.
